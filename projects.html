About Hobbies Projects

LED Sequencer PCB

A circuit that lights up 10 LEDs one after another in a chase pattern, with a knob to speed it up or slow it down. Built with a 555 timer and CD4017 counter, simulated in LTspice, and laid out as a two-layer PCB in Altium.

Robodog

A little robot that follows you around, keeping a steady distance using an ultrasonic sensor and motor control to track your movement.

Reveille: After Hours

A browser-based escape room game where our school mascot, Reveille, gets locked in the library and has to solve her way through a series of stages to get out.
